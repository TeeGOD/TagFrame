This is a glossary of all the lingo and signs and what they mean.

Key's signs

Signs on your punch and kicks called in order,
1,2,3,4 each reprensenting a limb

Directions pressed, clicked fast

Directions pressed, held

Anything in brackets means it's pressed in quick succession,
also written with ~ between the keys

WS

While Standing

WR

While Running also FFF

CD

Crouch Dash

SNK

Sneak

QCF, QCB

Quarter Circle Forward/Back

HCF, HCB

Half Circle Forward/Back

status effects

Bound, slames the opponent to the ground allowing you to keep your combo going.

Force Crouch, forces the opponent to be in a crouching state giving you better frame advantage

Homing, homes unto the opponent side movement also called tracking

FloorBreak, allows you to break the floor on the maps that have Breakable floors, uses your Bound

WallBreak, allows you to break the wall on the maps that have Breakable walls, uses your Bound

BalconyBreak, allows you to break the balcony on the maps that have Breakable balconies, uses your Bound
